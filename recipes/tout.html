Tout

Description

These fragrant, rose-scented, Persian-style marzipan sweets are part of the Sofre Haft-Sin - the traditional Norouz (Iranian New Year) display. "Tout" is the Farsi word for mulberry, and these sweets are traditionally shaped like mulberries.

Ingredients

  • Blanched almond flour - 1 cup
  • Powdered sugar - 1/2 cup
  • Ground cardamom - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Rose water - 2 tablespoons
  • White sugar - 1/4 cup
  • Raw pistachios - 2 tablespoons
